Science deserves thoughtful design.

Graph Solutions creates visual communication for biology, medicine and research. We collaborate with laboratories, universities, publishers and scientific organisations to transform complex information into clear, engaging and memorable experiences.

Whether developing an identity for an international bioinformatics resource or illustrating a biological pathway, our objective remains the same: helping science reach the widest possible audience without compromising accuracy.

Featured project

Reactome

Reactome is one of the world's leading open-access curated pathway databases, providing researchers with intuitive tools to visualise and analyse biological knowledge.

Our collaboration focused on redefining its visual identity while respecting the scientific rigour and openness that have made Reactome an essential resource for the international research community.

Beginning with ideas of layering, biological systems and interconnected knowledge, we developed a new visual language spanning branding, interface design, publications and digital communication.

Featured project

IntAct

IntAct is a freely available database providing molecular interaction data supported by experimental evidence and expert curation.

The identity draws inspiration from geometry, microscopic structures and the hidden architecture of biological systems.

Working closely with the IntAct team, we created a cleaner, stronger identity capable of expressing scientific precision while remaining approachable and memorable.
